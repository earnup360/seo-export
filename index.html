Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my YouTube SEO services.

How long does it take to see results from YouTube SEO?

Most clients start seeing improvements within 2-4 weeks, but significant results typically appear after 3-6 months as YouTube's algorithm needs time to recognize and reward your optimized content. The timeline depends on factors like your niche's competitiveness and how consistently you implement the strategies.

Do you work with channels in any niche?

Yes, I've worked with channels across dozens of niches including tech, beauty, gaming, finance, education, and more. However, I'm most experienced with informational and educational content rather than entertainment-focused channels.

What tools do you use for YouTube SEO?

I use a combination of specialized tools including TubeBuddy, VidIQ, Ahrefs, Google Keyword Planner, and YouTube Analytics. I also have proprietary methods developed through years of testing what works best for different types of channels.

Do you offer one-time optimization or ongoing services?

Both! I offer one-time video optimizations as well as monthly retainers for channels that want continuous optimization and strategy adjustments. Most growing channels benefit from ongoing support to maintain and improve their rankings.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing depends on your channel's size and needs. Basic video optimizations start at $50/video, while comprehensive channel packages begin at $300/month. Contact me for a free consultation and custom quote based on your specific goals.
